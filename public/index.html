Brazilian Beef Stroganoff

Preparation In a large sauce pan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil on high heat. Sear the meat for about one minute. Add the onions and garlic and cook for about 3 minutes. Add the 1⁄4 cup cognac and stir until it evaporates. Turn the heat down to medium and add the tomato paste, beef base, brown sugar, ketchup, and water. Cook about 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. In the meantime, heat the remaining olive oil in a medium skillet set over high heat, until smoking.
